### DISTRIBUTED SYSTEMS
## CHAPTER 03: PROCESSES

Processor:	Provides a set of instructions along with the capability of automatically executing a series of tose instructions.
			The minimal collection of values stored in the registers of a processor used for the execution of a series of instructions.

Thread:	A minimal software processos in whose context a series of instructions can be executed.
		The minimal collection of values stored in registers and memory, used for the execution of a series of instructions.

Process:	A software processor in whose context one or more threads may be executed.
